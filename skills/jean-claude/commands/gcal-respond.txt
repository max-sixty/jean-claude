Usage: jean-claude gcal respond [OPTIONS] EVENT_ID

  Respond to a calendar invitation.

  EVENT_ID: The event ID (from invitations or list output)

  Examples:
      jean-claude gcal respond EVENT_ID --accept
      jean-claude gcal respond EVENT_ID --decline --no-notify
      jean-claude gcal respond EVENT_ID --tentative

Options:
  --accept                Accept the invitation
  --decline               Decline the invitation
  --tentative             Tentatively accept
  --notify / --no-notify  Notify organizer (default: notify)
  --calendar TEXT         Calendar ID, email, or name (default: primary)
  --help                  Show this message and exit.
