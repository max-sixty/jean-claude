Usage: j-claude gdocs replace [OPTIONS] DOCUMENT_ID

  Find and replace text in a document.

  DOCUMENT_ID: The document ID (from the URL)

  Examples:     jean-claude gdocs replace 1abc...xyz --find "old text"
  --replace-with "new text"     jean-claude gdocs replace 1abc...xyz --find
  "TODO" --replace-with "DONE" --match-case

Options:
  --find TEXT          Text to find  [required]
  --replace-with TEXT  Replacement text  [required]
  --match-case         Case-sensitive matching
  --help               Show this message and exit.
