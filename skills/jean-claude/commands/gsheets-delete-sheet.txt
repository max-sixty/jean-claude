Usage: j-claude gsheets delete-sheet [OPTIONS] SPREADSHEET_ID SHEET_NAME

  Delete a sheet from a spreadsheet.

  SPREADSHEET_ID: The spreadsheet ID (from the URL) SHEET_NAME: Name of the
  sheet to delete

  Examples:     jean-claude gsheets delete-sheet SPREADSHEET_ID "Old Data"

Options:
  --help  Show this message and exit.
