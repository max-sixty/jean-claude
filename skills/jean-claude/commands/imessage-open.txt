Usage: j-claude imessage open [OPTIONS] CHAT_ID

  Open a chat in Messages.app (marks messages as read).

  CHAT_ID: The chat ID (e.g., any;-;+12025551234 or any;+;chat123...)

  Example:     jean-claude imessage open "any;-;+12025551234"     jean-claude
  imessage open "any;+;chat123456789"

Options:
  --help  Show this message and exit.
