Usage: jean-claude imessage send [OPTIONS] RECIPIENT

  Send an iMessage to a phone number, chat ID, or contact name.

  RECIPIENT: Phone number (+1234567890), chat ID (any;+;chat123...),
  email/Apple ID, or contact name.

  Message body is read from stdin.

  Examples:     echo "Hello!" | jean-claude imessage send "+12025551234"

      cat << 'EOF' | jean-claude imessage send "+12025551234"     It's great
      to hear from you!     EOF

Options:
  --help  Show this message and exit.
