Usage: jean-claude whatsapp unread [OPTIONS]

  List unread messages and auto-download images.

  Shows unread messages with sender, timestamp, and text content. Images are
  automatically downloaded to ~/.local/share/jean-claude/whatsapp/media/ and
  their file paths are included in the output.

  Examples:     jean-claude whatsapp unread     jean-claude whatsapp unread -n
  20

Options:
  -n, --max-results INTEGER  Maximum messages to return
  --help                     Show this message and exit.
